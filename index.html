Key Applications

01. Mission Readiness

Training and Simulation: VR and AR environments offer realistic training scenarios, allowing personnel to prepare for diverse combat situations in a controlled, cost-effective manner. Planning and Coordination: Advanced geospatial analytics aid in mission planning, providing detailed terrain analyses to strategize troop and equipment movements effectively.

02. Equipment Handling and Maintenance

Preventive Maintenance: Our state-of-the-art PMCS application enhances equipment readiness by providing soldiers with mobile access to technical manuals. This streamlines maintenance checks and fault reporting. Logistics Management: The DMC utilizes spatial computing to synchronize equipment distribution and manage inventories, ensuring readiness and reducing downtime.

03. Operational Efficiency

Real-Time Data Integration: By integrating real-time data from multiple sources, commanders receive a comprehensive battlefield overview, enabling quicker and more informed decision-making. Enhanced Communication: Spatial computing improves coordination across military branches, ensuring all units operate with up-to-date information.

Solutions

NAPCON

NAPCON, short for "Navigation, Planning, and Conquest," is an advanced software application designed for military training and strategic planning in virtual terrain warfare scenarios. It integrates cutting-edge technology such as virtual reality (VR) and geospatial data to provide a comprehensive platform for military personnel to navigate, strategize, and conquer in simulated environments. NAPCON enables users to analyze terrain, plan missions, and execute strategies with precision, offering real-time insights.

EKLAYVA

EKLAVYA is a pioneering VR training platform reshaping maintenance and repair training for A&B vehicles, aircraft, and heavy machinery. Employing a Four-Stage Training Model within immersive VR environments, it seamlessly integrates interactive simulations and assessments. With self-paced learning, trainees independently, advance mastering maintenance skills through hands- on practice and guided instruction, ensuring they're fully equipped and proficient for real-world scenarios with confidence and competence.
